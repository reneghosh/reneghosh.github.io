I am René Ghosh, a writer and musician from Rigaud, Québec, now living in Paris, France.

I write magical punk. My stories blend elements of sci-fi and the occasional supernatural element, but never occult the human stories that are my main focus. I try to maintain an authentic and genuine voice.
